Monad m => Alternative (Parser m a) Source #

Alternative instance using alt.

The "some" and "many" operations of alternative collect results in a pure list which is not scalable and streaming. Instead use the "splitParse" operation to apply a Parser repeatedly on a stream in a scalable manner.
